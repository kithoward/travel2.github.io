From the rainforests of the Amazon to the magical jungles of Borneo, there is a jungle destination for everyone!Take a look for some inspiration:

Most popular jungle destinations:

However, these are just some ideas. There are many more destinations around the world 
to see nature and wildlife in their natural jungle and rainforest environments. 
For example:

Madagascar

Sadly, Madagascar has been victim to much deforestation, however it is still a fantastic place to see beautiful rainforests. It is also home to the largest living lemurs left in the wild!

Rwanda

Rwanda is the place to see some of the last remaining mountain gorillas in a natural rainforest environment.

India

Home to the Roayl Bengal tiger, the jungles of India offer a once in a lifetime experience to witness these beutiful creatures.
